Roadmap

The GOV.UK Notify roadmap shows the things we’re working on and when we hope to have them ready for you to use.

The roadmap is a guide to what we have planned, but some things might change.

You can contact us if you have any questions about the roadmap or suggestions for new features.

March to June 2021

  • Increase the number of letters we can print each day
  • Let teams see how their organisation is using Notify
  • Introduce a simpler way to track the benefits of using Notify
  • Help teams to write better messages (discovery)

July to December 2021

  • Add multilingual letter templates so teams can provide information in several languages
  • Add large-print letter templates
  • Start sending emails from nhs.uk, scot.gov and parliament.uk email addresses
  • Help teams to write better messages (private beta)
  • Integrate Notify with the registry of protected and blocked text message sender names
  • Iterate Notify’s free message allowance model

January to March 2022

  • Help teams to write better messages (public beta)
  • Let teams add forms and attachments to letter templates
  • Explore ways to shorten links in emails and text messages
